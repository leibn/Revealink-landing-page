The $31.5 Billion "Knowledge Leak"

You've felt it. A key employee leaves, and suddenly, no one knows why a critical decision was made. That's 'Tacit Knowledge Leakage.' When an expert leaves your company, 42% of their unique, unwritten knowledge is lost forever. Existing tools find documents; they don't preserve expertise.

From a Static Library to a Living Boardroom

Standard knowledge tools (like RAG) are 'Static Libraries.' They give you a list of 10 documents and hope the answer is inside. Revealink is different. We build a 'Living Boardroom' for your company. Our Multi-Agent System creates 'Archived Agents' that learn from your experts and collaborate to synthesize specific, actionable answers.
